    ANG MGA KARAKTER NG NOLI ME TANGERE
    Kapitan Tiago
    		Kapitan Tiago is a prideful man. He is the father to Maria Clara, and has raised her with help from friends and family since her mother's death during childbirth. He does things solely for his benefit--such as indulging in polytheism in order to gain support from as many gods.
    Sisa
    		Sisa is a woeful and tragic character. She is the mother of Crispin and Basilio, and due to her race and gender, she is the at the bottom of the socioeconimic ladder of her time. She is trapped with a worthless no-good husband, and raises her kids on her sole shoulders.
    Philosopho Tasio
    		He is considered to be an eccentric old man for his disregard to the priests in power. He is however, religious in his own right. He simply does not approve of the power system. He is a wise man who read much in his free time to cope with the death of his wife.
